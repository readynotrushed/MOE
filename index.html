Scenario 1: student currently enrolled (2025/2026)

Exempt from new cut-off

Student enrolled in ANY school / nursery / ECC in 2025/2026
Includes public and private institutions
→ OLD CUT-OFF (31 Aug) applies indefinitely
Protected status: These students never fall under new cut-off, regardless of future transfers between schools or curricula
Scenario 2: student transferring (any year)

Academic continuity - transfers with existing cut-off

Student transferring between schools; between curricula; from abroad
Any academic year starting 2026/2027
→ ACADEMIC CONTINUITY applies
Transfer principle: Student transfers WITH their existing cut-off (old or new). The cut-off that applied in the previous school continues to apply. Academic progression continues: FS1/Pre-KG → FS2/KG1 → Year 1/KG2, regardless of which cut-off originally applied.
Scenario 3: children born September / December, turning 3, never enrolled, enrolling in British curriculum

Flexibility available - parents and school decide

Born: September / December of any year
Never enrolled in any school system before
Does NOT turn 3 by start of school year
Enrolling to British curriculum
→ SCHOOL + PARENTS DECIDE if ready for FS1
British curriculum flexibility: For children born in September / December enrolling in British curriculum, who do not turn 3 by start of the school year, parents and school jointly decide if ready for FS1. If deemed not ready, may join FS1 the following academic year.
BRITISH CURRICULUM SPECIAL CASES EXPLAINED. Applicable to children never enrolled in any school system, enrolling in British curriculum:
  • born September / December 2022:only in 2026/2027 as a one off exemption, parents + school can decide FS1 or FS2 for children turning 4 between September and December 2022
  • born September / December 2023:starting in 2026/2027, parents + school decide if FS1 now or delay to 2027/2028
  • born September / December in any future year:same flexibility applies: parents + school assess if FS1 when turning 3 (as in new cut-off) or delay to following year (as in old cut-off)
Scenario 4: child born September / December, turning 3, never enrolled, enrolling in IB / AMERICAN curriculum

New cut-off applies - no flexibility

Born: September / December of any year
Never enrolled in any school system before
Turning 3 after the start of the school year of the school
Enrolling to IB or AMERICAN curriculum
→ NEW CUT-OFF applies - NO flexibility
No Choice: IB and American curriculum schools must apply new cut-off strictly. Age by 31 December determines placement with no parental / school discretion.
Scenario 5: child never enrolled in any school system, enrolling in IB / American, starting 2026/2027

New cut-off applies - no flexibility

Child never enrolled in any school system before
Does not meet exemption criteria (not transferring, not previously enrolled)
Enrolling to IB or AMERICAN curriculum
→ NEW CUT-OFF applies - NO flexibility
Placement under new cut-off (IB / American):
  • 3 by 31 Dec → Pre-KG
  • 4 by 31 Dec → KG1
  • 5 by 31 Dec → KG2
  • 6 by 31 Dec → Grade 1

Critical case examples

Example 1: September / December 2022 child, never enrolled in school system
  • one-off exemption 2026/2027: Parents + school decide FS1 or FS2 only if enrolling in British curriculum (Scenario 3)
  • From 2027/2028 and beyond: one-off exemption doesn't apply anymore
Example 2: Born September 2023, never enrolled in school system, enrolling to BRITISH curriculum in 2026/2027
  • Will be 3 by 31 December 2026 → meets age requirement for FS1 in 2026/2027
  • Born September / December + enrolling in British → flexibility applies (Scenario 3)
  • Parents + school can choose: enrol in FS1 now (2026/2027), or delay FS1 to 2027/2028
Example 3: Born September 2023, never enrolled in school system, enrolling to IB / AMERICAN curriculum in 2026/2027
  • Will be 3 by 31 Dec 2026 → meets age requirement
  • Born September / December but enrolling in IB / American → NO flexibility (Scenario 4)
  • New cut-off applies strictly: 3 by 31 Dec 2026 → Pre-KG
Example 4: Born October 2022, never enrolled in school system, enrolling to BRITISH curriculum 2026/2027
  • Born September / December 2022 + enrolling in British → flexibility applies (Scenario 3)
  • Parents + school decide: FS1 or FS2 based on developmental readiness
Example 5: Born December 2023, in nursery 2025/2026, enrolling school 2026/2027
  • Was enrolled in nursery in 2025/2026 → exempt from new cut-off (Scenario 1)
  • Old cut-off applies: not yet 3 by start of school year → NO FS1 / NO Pre-KG in 2026/2027
  • Remains on old cut-off permanently
KEY PRINCIPLES
  • Once on old cut-off → always on old cut-off
  • Current enrolment (2025/2026) = permanent protection under old cut-off
  • Transfers = student transfers WITH their cut-off (old or new), academic continuity maintained
  • September / December students turning 3 enrolling in BRITISH curriculum and never enrolled in school system before = flexibility (parents + school decide)
  • September / December students turning 3 enrolling in IB/AMERICAN curriculum and never enrolled in school system before = new cut-off applies strictly, no flexibility
