Scenario 1: currently enrolled students (2025/2026)

Exempt from new cut-off

Student enrolled in ANY school/nursery/ECC in 2025/2026
Includes public and private institutions
→ OLD CUT-OFF (31 Aug) applies indefinitely
Protected Status: These students never fall under new cut-off, regardless of future transfers between schools or curricula
Scenario 2: students transferring (any year)

Academic continuity - transfers with existing cut-off

Student transferring between schools OR between curricula OR from abroad
Any academic year starting 2026/2027
→ ACADEMIC CONTINUITY applies
Transfer Principle: Student transfers WITH their existing cut-off (old or new). The cut-off that applied in the previous school continues to apply. Academic progression continues: FS1/Pre-KG → FS2/KG1 → Year 1/KG2, regardless of which cut-off originally applied.
Scenario 3: students born Sep-Dec turning 3, never enrolled, enrolling in BRITISH curriculum

Flexibility available - parents and school decide

Born: 1 Sep - 31 Dec of any year
Never enrolled in any school system before
Do NOT turn 3 by start of school year (late Aug/early Sep)
Enrolling to BRITISH curriculum
→ SCHOOL + PARENTS DECIDE if ready for FS1
British Curriculum Flexibility: For students born Sep-Dec enrolling in British curriculum, who do not turn 3 by start of the school year, parents and school jointly decide if ready for FS1. If deemed not ready, may join FS1 the following academic year.
Special Cases (students never enrolled in school system, enrolling in British curriculum):
  • Sep-Dec 2022 in 2026/2027: Will be 4 by 31 Dec 2026 but not by school start. Parents + school decide FS1 or FS2 only as a one-off exemption
  • Sep-Dec 2023 in 2026/2027: Will be 3 by 31 Dec 2026 but not by school start. Parents + school decide FS1 now or delay to 2027/2028
  • Sep-Dec births in any future year: Same flexibility applies when turning 3 and enrolling in British curriculum
Scenario 4: students born Sep-Dec turning 3, never enrolled, enrolling in IB/AMERICAN curriculum

New cut-off applies - no flexibility

Born: 1 Sep - 31 Dec of any year
Never enrolled in any school system before
Do NOT turn 3 by start of school year (late Aug/early Sep)
Enrolling to IB or AMERICAN curriculum
→ NEW CUT-OFF (31 Dec) applies - NO flexibility
No Choice: IB and American curriculum schools must apply new cut-off strictly. Age by 31 December determines placement with no parental/school discretion.
Scenario 5: all other students never enrolled (IB/American schools - every year from 2026/2027)

New cut-off applies - no flexibility

Student never enrolled in any school system before
Does not meet exemption criteria (not transferring, not previously enrolled)
Enrolling to IB or AMERICAN curriculum
→ NEW CUT-OFF (31 Dec) applies - NO flexibility
Placement under new cut-off (IB/American):
  • 3 by 31 Dec → Pre-KG
  • 4 by 31 Dec → KG1
  • 5 by 31 Dec → KG2
  • 6 by 31 Dec → Grade 1

Critical case examples

Example 1: Sep-Dec 2022 child, never enrolled in school system
  • For 2026/2027: Parents + school decide FS1 or FS2 only if enrolling in British curriculum (Scenario 3)
  • For 2027/2028 and beyond: Would have aged out, this special case doesn't apply
Example 2: Born Sep 2023, never enrolled in school system, enrolling to BRITISH curriculum 2026/2027
  • Will turn 3 in Sep 2026 (AFTER school starts in late Aug)
  • Will be 3 by 31 Dec 2026 → meets age requirement for FS1 in 2026/2027
  • Born Sep-Dec + enrolling in British → flexibility applies (Scenario 3)
  • Parents + school can choose: enrol in FS1 now (2026/2027), OR delay to 2027/2028
Example 3: Born Sep 2023, never enrolled in school system, enrolling to IB/AMERICAN curriculum 2026/2027
  • Will turn 3 in Sep 2026 (after school starts)
  • Will be 3 by 31 Dec 2026 → meets age requirement
  • Born Sep-Dec but enrolling in IB/American → NO flexibility (Scenario 4)
  • New cut-off applies strictly: 3 by 31 Dec 2026 → Pre-KG
Example 4: Born Oct 2022, never enrolled in school system, enrolling to BRITISH curriculum 2026/2027
  • Born Sep-Dec 2022 + enrolling in British → flexibility applies (Scenario 3)
  • Parents + school decide: FS1 or FS2 based on developmental readiness
Example 5: Born Dec 2023, in nursery 2025/2026, enrolling school 2026/2027
  • Was enrolled in nursery in 2025/2026 → exempt from new cut-off (Scenario 1)
  • Old cut-off applies: not yet 3 by start of school year → NO FS1 / NO Pre-KG in 2026/2027
  • Remains on old cut-off permanently
Key principles:
  • Once on old cut-off (31 Aug) → always on old cut-off
  • Current enrolment (2025/2026) = permanent protection under old cut-off
  • Transfers = student transfers WITH their cut-off (old or new), academic continuity maintained
  • Sep-Dec students turning 3 enrolling in BRITISH curriculum and never enrolled in school system before = flexibility (parents + school decide)
  • Sep-Dec students turning 3 enrolling in IB/AMERICAN curriculum and never enrolled in school system before = new cut-off applies strictly, no flexibility
